PlainPad Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Overview

PlainPad is a plain text editor for macOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data PlainPad does and does not collect.

Data Collection

PlainPad does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no network connections of any kind.

Data Storage

All notes and text files are stored locally on your Mac only. PlainPad stores your open tabs and preferences in your Mac's local Application Support folder. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

PlainPad does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.
